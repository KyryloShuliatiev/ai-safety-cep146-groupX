## Why AI Safety Matters
- Rapid advancement of large-scale AI models.
- Governments organizing AI Safety Summits (UK, US, EU).
- Companies like OpenAI, Google, Meta, Anthropic signing safety agreements.

## Key Risks
1. Deepfakes affecting elections.
2. Biological threat creation through AI models.
3. Autonomous agents executing harmful tasks.
4. Model hallucinations causing misinformation.

## Global AI Safety Actions
- AI Safety Institute (UK)
- US Executive Order on AI (2023)
- Frontier Model Safety Commitments
- EU AI Act
